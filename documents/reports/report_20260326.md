## 5. Key Findings

1. **Jul remains the best single date** — A_v2_Jul SegFormer (0.1816) confirms that the original Exp A date selection was correct. Even multi-temporal Phase B peaks at 0.1355, still below A_v2_Jul.

2. **Phase B elbow at k=3 (147ch)** — best trade-off between channels and performance. k=4,5 add 42–65 more channels with marginal or negative gain.

3. **Phase A saturates at k=5 (39ch)** with test_mIoU=0.1305 — approaches but doesn't beat single-date (0.1816). Band diversity on per-crop top dates alone is insufficient.

4. **CBAM advantage in Phase B** — DeepLabV3+CBAM overtakes SegFormer when many dates are included (k≥2), suggesting channel attention effectively filters temporal noise.

5. **Jan window is harmful** — training on dormant-season imagery degrades performance severely (0.0215). Should not be used as sole input.

6. **Mar partially validates phenological hypothesis** — Winter Wheat IoU in A_v2_Mar (13.40%) exceeds A_v2_Jul (13.24%), confirming that spring window is marginally better for winter crops as expected.
